Our Journey

Nada Bramha means "The World is Sound". We believe that music is the easiest path to reach the divine. Established in Sydney, our group brings together individuals from diverse backgrounds united by a love for Bhajans, Abhangs, and Kirtans.

Our mission is to create an atmosphere of peace and spiritual tranquility wherever we perform. We invite you to close your eyes, listen to the rhythm of the manjira, the melody of the harmonium, and find the silence within the sound.
